Welcome

The Alpha Alumni Association welcome's you to a night of recognition and Award. Along with presenting the annual Brotherhood Scholarship Award, this year we are proud to announce our inaugural Quete Recognition and Scholarship Award. The Quete Award will be given to an exceptional undergraduate student who supports and promotes the vision of Sigma Lambda Beta to better our communities and the world through education.
