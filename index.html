Welcome

This year, along with our annual Brotherhood Scholarship which awards an undergraduate brother, the AAA is proud to announce its inaugural Quete award recognition and scholarship. The Quete scholarship will be awarded to an exceptional University of Iowa undergraduate student that supports and promotes Sigma Lambda Beta's vision to better our communities and the world through their educational journey.
